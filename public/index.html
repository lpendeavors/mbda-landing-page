The Houston Minority Business Development Agency (MBDA) Business Center is operated by Houston Community College and is funded, in part, by the U.S. Department of Commerce through the Minority Business Development Agency.


OUR SPEAKERS


Misty Starks

Misty Starks is an award-winning writer and video producer, publicity consultant, technology advocate, and founder of Misty Blue Media, a digital publishing and content creation firm. She is also President of 3B Resources Group Public Relations, LLC. Prior to her work in corporate America, Misty was a producer for the internationally syndicated television program, Judge Alex. She was also Web producer for Black Entertainment Television (BET).

Centrell Reed

Centrell Reed is a Houston-based entrepreneur, business leader, ACTION Coach, speaker, media figure, and philanthropist who is passionate about building foundations for growth, wellness, and prosperity. Centrell is the owner of CReed Global Media & Production (CGM) in Houston, TX. Often compared to Oprah, Michelle Obama, Kerry Washington Angela Bassett, and other well-known figures, Centrell is forging her own path in the media world by providing opportunities for everyone to WIN. She hosts The Centrell Show in her own 4,000 square foot CReed Global Studios with thousands of viewers worldwide.
